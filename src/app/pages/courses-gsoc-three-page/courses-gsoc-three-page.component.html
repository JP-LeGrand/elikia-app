Overview

For Children and Teenagers

We guide children and teenagers in learning French through programs tailored to their age, level, and objectives:

  • Structured French Courses: Gradually build your oral and written skills to develop a strong and lasting foundation in French.
  • Exam and Certification Preparation: Prepare for international French exams and certifications: DELF, IB, GCSE, and more.
  • French Tutoring and Academic Support: Support from kindergarten through high school for students enrolled in French schools or following the French national curriculum.
  • Fun Clubs and Interactive Activities: Participate in engaging activities that make learning French motivating and dynamic.
  • Language Immersion: Strengthen your French through workshops, games, and daily practice opportunities.
Overview

For Adults

Our adult programs are designed to help you achieve personal, professional, or academic goals:

  • Conversational French: Gain confidence and fluency to communicate effectively in everyday situations.
  • General French Courses: Progress at your own pace according to the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (A1 to C2).
  • Certification Preparation: Prepare for official exams: DELF, TEF, TCF, tailored to your study, career, or relocation projects.
  • Personalized Courses: Customized programs designed to meet your specific goals.
Overview

For Companies

We offer professional French training programs designed for teams and businesses:

  • Business French: Master French to collaborate effectively with partners in Francophone Africa.
  • French for Meetings and Negotiations: Build the vocabulary and skills needed for professional interactions.
  • French for Business Travel: Practical training for assignments and trips across Francophone Africa.
  • Custom Programs: Tailored courses to fit your industry and your team’s specific needs.
Overview

Training Formats

Our courses are flexible and accessible:

  • In-person in Johannesburg and Pretoria
  • Online, from anywhere
  • Individual or group sessions
  • Programs for children, adults, and corporate teams
